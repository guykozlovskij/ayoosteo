DSE Workstation Assessment

Many musculoskeletal pain cases are caused by either a poor workstation set up or poor use of a good workstation setup.

A large portion of how we spend our days is spend behind a computer screen for many of us.

Who needs to be assessed:

  • Anyone who works behind a desk for an hour or more at a time
  • New Employees
  • New changes to your workstation
  • Anyone complaining of discomfort/pain
  • Working from home

In Britain the average employee work for 34 hours and 26 minutes a week, adding up to a total of 1,795 hours a year, and 84,365 hours over a lifetime.
